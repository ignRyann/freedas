Background History

Our facials are customized, luxurious treatments that re-hydrate and rejuvenate your skin, restoring the glowing complexion you deserve. The process involves massaging the face and neck, steaming the face, exfoliation, extraction of blackheads and whiteheads, and application of a face pack to rejuvenate the skin, making it look healthier and youthful. Some advantages of facials include preventing the formation of wrinkles and aging lines, cleansing the skin, increasing blood circulation, relaxing the nerves, strengthening weak muscle tissues, and improving skin texture and complexion, adding to your confidence.
