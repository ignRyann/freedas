Background History

Eyelash and Eyebrow tinting involves carefully dying the lashes with safe, high-quality tints, to give them a natural, luscious, darker appearance. Various eyelash and eyebrow tint colors (including blues, browns, blacks, and greys) are available to complement your skin and hair coloring perfectly. Eyelash tinting, using "Strictly professional" from the UK, creates definition and the appearance of longer, thicker lashes for a natural look. Eyebrows Tinting, using Sexy Brow Henna, provides a natural dye with a tattoo effect for up to 2 months, creating a beautiful "powder eyebrows" effect. The process is hygienic with 97% natural components.
