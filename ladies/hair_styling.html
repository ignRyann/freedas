Background History

Our hair styling services use traditional Indian herbs from henna plants to color and condition your hair. Herbal henna provides a reddish-brown color, blending grey hair with natural dark hair and giving it a highlighted effect. It also acts as a conditioner, adding extra shine and luster. Herbal hair color contains natural henna and herbs, with minimal chemicals, to provide a natural look. Hair coloring can significantly enhance your appearance and spirit with various options available. We offer various haircuts to suit your personality, along with shampoo and blow-dry services to complete your look.
