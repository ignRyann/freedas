Service Information

  • Service: Hands & Feet Treatment
  • Types: Manicure, Pedicure, Soak-Off Gel Nail, Paraffin Wax Treatment

Background History

Our hands and feet treatments include manicures and pedicures which involve filing, shaping of the nails, treatments, and massage, along with the application of polish. Soak-off gel nails are a flexible and lightweight option that uses UV light to cure each layer, making them look natural. Paraffin wax treatments are also available to soften and moisturize dry, cracked skin by retaining body oils and removing dead skin cells, leaving the skin smoother and healthier.
